Pistachio Bundt Cake with Cream Cheese Glaze

Description

If you're looking for a tasty dessert recipe, you have to try this pistachio cake. It is moist and delicious and the cream cheese glaze is an added bonus. And if you arenâ€™t a pistachio fan, you can make this and substitute the pudding with another flavor just as easily. Like lemon bundt cake, strawberry bundt cake, etc.

Ingredients

  • Bundt cake
    • 15.25 oz yellow cake mix (without pudding)
    • 3 oz Instant Pistachio pudding mix
    • 4 eggs
    • 1 cup water
    • 1/4 cup oil
  • Optional Cream Cheese Glaze
    • 4 oz cream cheese (at room temperature)
    • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
    • 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
    • 3 tbsp milk
    • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Cake
    1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
    2. Blend all ingredients together for two minutes with electric mixer or Kitchenaid.
    3. Pour into greased bundt pan.
    4. Bake in pre-heated oven for 45 minutes.
    5. Cool cake.
    6. Turn out onto cake plate.
    7. Optional - dust with powdered sugar or Cream Cheese Glaze
  2. Cream Cheese Glaze (Optional)
    1. In the bowl of an electric mixer fitted with the paddle attachment, beat cream cheese on medium-high speed until light and fluffy, 4 to 5 minutes, scraping down sides of bowl as needed.
    2. Add sugar and beat until combined, about 1 minute.
    3. Add salt, lemon juice, and milk; mix on low speed until smooth. If glaze is too thick to drizzle, add more milk, 1 tablespoon at a time.
    4. Slowly and evenly drizzle the glaze onto the cake, and then let stand before serving.
